Game Overview

Drivemad delivers intense racing action with:

  • Realistic Physics

    Advanced vehicle dynamics simulation

How to Play

PC Controls

  • WASD/Arrow keys - Steering
  • Space - Handbrake
  • Shift - Nitro Boost

Mobile Tips

  • Tilt device for steering
  • Tap left/right for controls
  • Pinch to zoom camera
